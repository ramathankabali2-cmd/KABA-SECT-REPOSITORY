How much does a website cost?

The cost depends on the number of pages, features, design level, and support needed. Simple websites start from UGX 250,000, while business or school websites may cost more.

How long does it take to build a website?

A simple website can take a few days if all content is ready. Larger websites may take longer depending on the pages, features, photos, and client feedback.

Yes. We can help with updates, small improvements, corrections, performance checks, and general website support after the website is completed.
